Rich Cultural Heritage

Experience the vibrant cultural tapestry that makes Andhra Pradesh unique.

Kuchipudi Dance

Originating from the village of Kuchipudi, this classical dance form is known for its graceful movements and expressive storytelling.

Kalamkari Art

A traditional art form that involves hand-painting or block-printing on cotton or silk fabric using natural dyes.

Andhra Cuisine

Famous for its spicy and flavorful dishes, including Pulihora, Gongura Pachadi, and the iconic Andhra-style Biryani.

Vibrant Festivals

Experience the colorful celebrations that showcase the rich traditions of Andhra Pradesh.

Sankranti

A harvest festival celebrated in January with colorful rangoli, kite flying, and traditional sweets like Pongali and Ariselu.

January

Ugadi

The Telugu New Year celebrated with special dishes like Ugadi Pachadi, which combines six different flavors representing life's emotions.

March/April

Brahmotsavam

A nine-day festival at Tirumala Temple featuring elaborate processions, cultural performances, and religious ceremonies.

September/October
